Q. Tell me the file that starts with `c`.  <br>What is the purpose of that file

>A. `c` Are special or device files. Represents hardware devices, any hardware E.g. keyboard or mouse connected to the machine will generate the corresponding file which is labeled as `c`

Q. Tell me the identification letter of the device file. <br> Where are they located?

> Identification letter of device file is `c`. They are located in `/dev`
