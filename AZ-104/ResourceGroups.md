<h2>Resource Group </h2>

![](images/2026-03-29-16-16-48.png)


- Resource Group is a container where you put all your resources, something like a folder.

- Used with RBAC (Role Based Access Control) to control the permissions and actions that are allowed in the group.

- <span style="color:red">DELETING THE RESROUCE GROUP WILL DELETE ALL THE RESOURCES INSIDE IT!!!</span>

- Benefits of Resource Groups
  - 1. Organisation for easier management
  - 2. Easy deprovisioning, just by deleteing the resource group you can delete all at once.
  - 3. Security Boundary by utilizing RBAC
  - 4. Easy to apply policies.
