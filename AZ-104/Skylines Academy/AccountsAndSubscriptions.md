<h2>Azure Account Hierarchy </h2>

![](images/image.png)

Azure Enterprise Agreement
> Designed for large enterprises to segment costs and manage large-scale subscription creation <br><br>
> Allows Administration of things like Departments and Accounts.

Azure Subscription
> Place where user manage their resources

Resource Groups
> Container / folder for managing resources

<br><br>
![](images/2026-03-29-15-32-03.png)
Management Groups
> Contianer / Groups that is used to segregate users or subscriptions. <br><br>
> This is to help administrators to better delegate access (Role Based Access Controls) and policies down the hierachy
