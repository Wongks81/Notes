<h2> Regions and Availability Zones </h2>

Azure Regions:
- Locations where resources are saved
- Area contains at least one datacenter
- When deploying a resource, region will need to selected.

Geographies:
- Boundaries, often tag to country borders
- Normally 2 or more regions in a geography for data preservation
- Depending on intended audience, you will need to take note of the compliance needs in the geography.<br><br>
 E.g. Deploying Non English instructions to English speaking countries.

- There is also a need to take note of any Data requirements in the region to meet your needs.
- Is fault tolerant as there are more than 1 region for data recovery
- <b>EACH REGION</b> belogns to a single geography
