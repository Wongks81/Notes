## Adding Domain to tenant

1. On the Azure main page, click on Entra ID 

  ![](images/2026-05-16-09-38-21.png)

2. On the right, under basic information, you will see your Primary Domain name

  ![](images/2026-05-16-09-41-05.png)

3. Look for Custom Domain names on the left

  ![](images/2026-05-16-09-42-53.png)

4. Click on "Add custom domain" on the sub menu bar

  ![](images/2026-05-16-09-43-44.png)

5. Enter the Domain Name that you have and click "Add domain" at the bottom.

  ![](images/2026-05-16-09-44-58.png)

6. You have to add either a TXT or MX record to verify the domain

  ![](images/2026-05-16-09-45-50.png)


7. Access the Domain Name Registrars like Namecheap, GoDaddy or Cloudflare

8. Using step 6 as a reference add the TXT record in it.

  ![](images/2026-05-16-09-49-08.png)

9. Click on "Verify" on the bottom.

  > Depending on Microsoft Servers, it might take from a few minutes to a day just to verify this domain.

10. Now that you should see you custom domain name in the list

  ![](images/2026-05-16-09-52-58.png)

11. To make it the Primary Domain, click on the domain row

12. On this page you can click on "Make Primary" to make it the primary domain.

  ![](images/2026-05-16-09-54-16.png)
